Welcome to GAC SMHI Weather Solutions™ Report Creator! This tool will help you fill in and send status reports during your voyages.

Reports should normally be sent on voyages at least4 daysin length, unless any other minimum duration is agreed upon.

Please choose a report type above to create a report.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Daily reports that GAC SMHI Weather Solutions™ receive from ships all over the world form an integral part of the complex calculations that produce forecasts and recommendations for vessels traveling the globe. Hence, your efforts to provide us with high quality data are appreciated.
